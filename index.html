2600 BCE
Indus Valley Civilization begins, with advanced urban planning in cities like Harappa and Mohenjo-Daro.
1500 BCE
Vedic Period starts, with the composition of the Rigveda and the arrival of Indo-Aryans.
563 BCE
Birth of Gautama Buddha, founder of Buddhism, in Lumbini.
327 BCE
Alexander the Great invades northwest India, leading to cultural exchanges.
321 BCE
Chandragupta Maurya founds the Maurya Empire, unifying most of India.
185 BCE
End of the Maurya Empire; Shunga Empire rises.
320 CE
Gupta Empire begins, often considered a golden age of Indian culture and science.
1206 CE
Delhi Sultanate established, marking the start of Muslim rule in India.
1526 CE
Babur founds the Mughal Empire after the First Battle of Panipat.
1858 CE
British Crown takes direct control of India after the Sepoy Mutiny of 1857.
1947 CE
India gains independence from British rule; partition creates Pakistan.
